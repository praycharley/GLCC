About Global Light Charismatic Chapel

Global Light Charismatic Chapel (GLCC) is a faith-driven Christian ministry committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transforming lives through the Word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit. Founded on a vision to illuminate the world with God’s truth, GLCC provides a vibrant and welcoming environment for believers to grow spiritually and connect with a supportive community.

Our Mission

The mission of the ministry is souls.(thus,winning souls from every kindred, every nation and every people), with a vision of making a global impact through the expression of God’s love.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope, love, and transformation in our community and beyond, demonstrating the grace and truth of God to all people.

Core Beliefs

  • The Bible: We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, guiding our faith and practice.
  • Jesus Christ: We affirm Jesus as the Savior of the world and the foundation of our faith.
  • The Holy Spirit: We rely on the power of the Holy Spirit for guidance, transformation, and spiritual growth.

What We Offer

  • Inspirational Worship Services: Experience uplifting praise and worship that draws believers closer to God.
  • Biblical Teachings: Receive life-transforming sermons grounded in Scripture.
  • Global Jesus Outreach: Raising Kingdom Leaders, Kingdom Financiers, Kingdom Intercessors, and Vibrant Ministers.
  • Youth and Children’s Ministry: Empower the next generation with faith and purpose.
  • Discipleship Programs: Grow spiritually through Bible study, mentorship, and prayer groups.

Join Us

Whether you are seeking a deeper relationship with God, spiritual growth, or a loving community to belong to, Global Light Charismatic Chapel welcomes you with open arms. Together, we can illuminate the world with the light of Christ.
