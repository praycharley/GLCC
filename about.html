Global Light Charismatic Chapel

The church was founded by Pastor Stephen Owusu Badu in 2022, February 20th, with about 50 founding members. The church currently has its headquarters in Obuasi, New Baakoyeden, with an average membership of 200. Another church branch, Wellspring assembly with an average membership of 50 at Asonkore, Obuasi. The GLCC was birthed out of the non-denominational Global Jesus Outreach ministry and now serves as its overarching umbrella. The church's mandate is to spread the light of the gospel for global impact and to stimulate the sight of the world to see the Lord Jesus. Our way of life is of holiness and purity

Our Mission
